DISEASE DISCOVERY


Aim:

First player to get three pieces to the opposite 'Home' side wins.


Instructions:

In each turn, players can move their pieces 3 times. Moves can be split amongst any of their pieces in play in whichever way they like: one piece 3 times, or 3 different pieces each once.

Pieces can move forward, or sideways if an adjacent square has a symbol that matches their current square. Pieces cannot move backwards.

If a player encounters a piece of the other player, neither piece can move forward and may be blocked if they can't move sideways either.

Within the 'Free' lane, a player can move their piece to any unoccupied square.

Players can only land on the 'Home' square if it is unoccupied by the other player.
